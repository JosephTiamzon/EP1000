What is the purpose of this website?

This website is to document and record the efforts I have made during my time in EP1000 from October 17 2022 to February 13 2023.


What is EP1000 about?

EP1000(Digital Fabrication & Prototyping Fundementals) is an elective module, along with EP1001 and EP1002, that allows students to develop design thinking and prototyping skills. Within this elective, students get to apply design and digital fabrication process for developing a functional prototype. In addition, students get to learn about the CDIO initiative (Conceive – Design – Implement – Operate) and apply it towards their project.
