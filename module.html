Why did I take EP1000?

EP1000(Digital Fabrication & Prototyping Fundementals) allows me to further improve my development skills. With the skills acquired, I hope to apply it towards my hobbies of cosplaying and figure-making.

From this module, I hope to further expand my understanding of:

  • 3D designing
  • 3D printing
  • Art engraving using laser cutter
  • electronic implementation

I hope to slowly improve and build my skills to a level as Iron Man cosplayers that incorporates electronics to their cosplay to bring it to life.
